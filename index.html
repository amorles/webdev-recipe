HOW TO CRAFT AN ENCHANTED GOLDEN APPLE

Only works on old versions of Minecraft PC

Ingredients

  • 72x gold ingots
  • 1x apple

Method

  1. Mine 72 blocks of gold ore.
  2. Smelt 72 blocks of gold ore into 72 gold ingots.
  3. Using 72 gold ingots, craft 8 blocks of gold using a 3x3 formation in the crafting table
  4. Retrieve 1 apple.
  5. Put 1 apple in the center of the crafting table. surround it with 8 blocks of gold, like how a pack of lions surround a zebra. Leave no room for escape.
  6. Profit!
